How we work

Appeared a new need of a particular technical expert or support? Share it with us!

First, we conduct a call to understand a client's need and request. When we define the needs and current challenges, we are able to suggest a good fit team member with relevant experience.

During up to three days, we come back to you with our portfolio with the best match talents to consider and choose then. You will give us feedback based on the CVs as well as a time slot to have an intro call. All our developers will join your engineering team within 4 weeks.

Once you approve the candidate, we prepare the agreement and discuss the final frames and conditions to work. Afterwards, when the contract is agreed - congratulations, the agreement with DevKit is signed and a new software talent joins your team!
